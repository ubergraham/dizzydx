Episodic vestibular syndromes may present as isolated dizziness, but more often present with other symptoms as well. .

Arrhythmias can present as episodic dizziness, but often will have additional cardiac symptoms: syncope or near-syncope, palpitations, etc. These patients typically require cardiac evaluation and monitoring.

Posterior circulation TIAs can certainly present this way as well, and should be considered in patients with clear episodes of dizziness (and typically with cardiac and/or thromboembolic risk factors).

Like all migraines, vestibular migraines occur episodically, but will present with dizziness along with headache. They can be treated typical migraine medications.
