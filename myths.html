Myths

  • Isolated dizziness = benign
  • Lightheadedness is less likely to be stroke
  • Exacerbation with head movement = benign
  • Negative CT rules out stroke

Pitfalls

  • Reliance on “type” of dizzy
  • Neglecting timing and triggers
  • Neglecting hallmark eye findings
  • Reliance on age, vascular risk factors
  • Reliance on head CT
